Project Overview

The staff that work the front desks at NIU's residence halls perform a variety of important tasks, from registering guests to loaning out temporary room keys to residents. When I sat down with NIU's front desk manager, I was shocked to learn how much time and energy was wasted tracking packages manually. Desk staff were required to write down the package's information, not once, but twice! Multiple-hour blocks were set aside solely for processing of newly delivered packages. To solve this time wasting, I designed and led a team of developers to implement the package system that is now in use in all of NIU's residence halls.
